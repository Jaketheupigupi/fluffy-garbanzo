Reduce Your Carbon Footprint

Introduction


A carbon footprint refers to the total amount of greenhouse gases released into the atmosphere as a result of human activities. These activities include things like using electricity, driving vehicles, producing goods, and even the food we eat. Every person, company, and country contributes to carbon emissions in some way, whether directly or indirectly. Understanding your carbon footprint is important because it helps show how much impact your lifestyle has on the environment. By being aware of it, we can start making smarter choices to reduce pollution and protect the planet for the future.



What is Carbon Footprint?

Carbon footprint is caused by many human activities such as transportation, electricity use, production, and daily lifestyle choices.
